Stress Diversion / by Megg

What we do:

We offer a literary way to escape a hard day or even a difficult time. Sure as heck beats falling during the tree pose in yoga.

There's so many things going on in our lives, and sometimes the stress can bring upon feelings of self-doubt or inadequacy which may lead to depression. It takes five seconds to share something motivating (and forever to get that damn kink out your neck)

Our mission:

to provide a forum for everyone to go to when they may be going through anything from job issues to a bad case of the blues.
